About MAJER ENTERPRISES

Reliable • Affordable • Quality Printing Solutions

MAJER ENTERPRISES is a trusted supplier of high-quality printer cartridges, toner, and office supplies, dedicated to supporting businesses, institutions, and individuals with reliable and cost-effective printing solutions.

With operations across Kenya and South Sudan, we have established ourselves as a dependable partner for organizations that require consistent performance and uninterrupted printing services. Our focus is on delivering products that meet high standards while maintaining affordability and accessibility.

We understand how critical printing is to daily operations, which is why we prioritize fast response, product availability, and customer satisfaction. Whether serving small offices or large institutions, we aim to provide solutions that improve efficiency and reduce downtime.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable printing supplies that enable businesses and individuals to operate efficiently without interruptions.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of printing solutions in East Africa by delivering reliability, value, and excellent customer service.
